About Les projos de l'ENSIM

Located in the beautiful city of Le Mans, the city of rillettes!

Our reels have been spinning since 1994. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is our pleasure!

Accomodations

Our theaters are equipped with the latest technology, ensuring an immersive cinematic experience that will transport you to another world.

Our comfortable seats are designed to cradle your body, providing the perfect combination of support and relaxation. We also offer a variety of gourmet snacks and beverages to satisfy your cravings during the film.

Whether you're looking for a romantic date night or a fun outing with friends, our cinema accommodations are the perfect choice.
